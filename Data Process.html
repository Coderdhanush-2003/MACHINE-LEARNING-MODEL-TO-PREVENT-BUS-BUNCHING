General Architecture
Description:

A supervised learning model trained on this rich data landscape predicts the likelihood of bus bunching. Think of it as a fortune teller for traffic flow, anticipating where and when buses might clump together. Armed with these predictions, the system triggers real-time interventions. Buses approaching potential bunching zones might be gently held at stops, their speeds adjusted, or even rerouted to avoid congestion. The system seamlessly integrates with existing infrastructure. Bus dispatch systems receive instructions, passenger information systems update arrival times, and traffic management systems adjust signal timing to prioritize the smooth flow of buses.Performance metrics like prediction accuracy and headway adherence are constantly monitored. The model continuously learns and adapts, incorporating new data and refining its strategies to maintain its edge against the ever-changing choreography of city traffic.
